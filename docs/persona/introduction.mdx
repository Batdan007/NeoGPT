Imagine personas as characters that NeoGPT adopts to understand and respond better in diverse situations. It's like assigning distinct roles to the model, adding variety and interest to your conversations. NeoGPT can embody multiple personas, each with its unique role and style, enhancing the diversity and engagement in your chats.


We have designed a set of personas that you can use to personalize your chat. You can choose the one that best suits your use case. You can also create your own personas so NeoGPT can adopt to your specific needs.
